What We Do

Around one billion people worldwide don't own a pair of shoes, leaving them vulnerable to injury, infection, and barriers to education and work. So we organize shoe drives to collect new and gently-used shoes for our local community in Canton, Michigan, and abroad in Guatemela. Our mission is to ensure comfort, dignity, and opportunity in every step. We are a registered state-certified non-profit organization.


To date, we have collected and donated over 6,000 shoes, including having donated to 5+ separate villages in Guatemala, each with separate needs for different footwear and types of shoes!
